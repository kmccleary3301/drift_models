# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We are committed to a respectful, constructive, and evidence-oriented community.

Participants are expected to:
- engage professionally,
- critique ideas and implementations (not people),
- avoid harassment, hate speech, and personal attacks,
- avoid misleading claims about results or provenance.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, intimidation, or discriminatory language.
- Doxxing or privacy violations.
- Repeated bad-faith disruption.
- Deliberate misrepresentation of experimental results.

## Reporting

Please report incidents to maintainers via private issue or security contact channel listed in `SECURITY.md`.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content, restrict participation, or ban accounts for violations.
